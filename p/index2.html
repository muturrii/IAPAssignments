Overview

Andrew Garfield is a British-American actor born on August 20, 1983. He gained widespread recognition for his performances in both film and theater. Garfield's breakthrough came with his portrayal of Eduardo Saverin in the 2010 film "The Social Network," which earned him critical acclaim.

  One of his most notable roles is that of Peter Parker/Spider-Man in the "The Amazing Spider-Man" film series (2012 and 2014). Garfield's charismatic and nuanced portrayal of the iconic superhero garnered him a large fan base.

Education

Andrew Garfield was born on August 20, 1983, in Los Angeles, California, but he was raised in Surrey, England. He developed an early interest in acting and attended the City of London Freemen's School, where he actively participated in school productions. After completing his secondary education, he pursued acting further.

  Garfield then enrolled at the Central School of Speech and Drama in London, a renowned performing arts institution. He graduated in 2004 with a Bachelor of Arts in Acting. His time at drama school played a crucial role in honing his acting skills and laying the foundation for his successful career in both theater and film. Following his education, Andrew Garfield began his journey as a professional actor, eventually achieving widespread acclaim for his performances in various mediums.


Career

Andrew Garfield's career has spanned film, television, and theater, showcasing his versatility as an actor. Here's a brief overview of his career:

  1. **Early Career and Television:**
     Andrew Garfield began his career in theater, earning recognition for his stage performances. He made his television debut in the UK series "Sugar Rush" in 2005 and appeared in other shows like "Doctor Who" before transitioning to film.
  
  2. **Breakthrough with "The Social Network" (2010):**
     Garfield gained widespread acclaim for his role as Eduardo Saverin in the critically acclaimed film "The Social Network," directed by David Fincher. This performance marked a breakthrough in his career and earned him several award nominations.
  
  3. **"The Amazing Spider-Man" Series (2012-2014):**
     Andrew Garfield played the iconic role of Peter Parker/Spider-Man in Marc Webb's "The Amazing Spider-Man" and its sequel. While the films received mixed reviews, Garfield's portrayal of the beloved superhero was generally well-received by audiences.
  
  4. **Critical Acclaim in "Hacksaw Ridge" (2016):**
     Garfield received widespread praise for his role as Desmond Doss in Mel Gibson's war drama "Hacksaw Ridge." His performance earned him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor.
  
  5. **Theater Success:**
     Alongside his film career, Andrew Garfield has been active in theater. He notably starred in the National Theatre's production of "Angels in America," earning a Tony Award for Best Actor in a Play in 2018. His commitment to stage work has solidified his reputation as a talented actor across different mediums.
  
  6. **Diverse Film Roles:**
     Garfield continued to take on diverse roles in films such as "Silence" (2016), directed by Martin Scorsese, and "Under the Silver Lake" (2018). These roles showcased his range and ability to tackle complex characters.
  
  7. **Ongoing Career:**

His commitment to both mainstream and independent films, coupled with his success in theater, has established him as a respected and versatile actor in the entertainment industry.
