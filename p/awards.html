Awards

As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, Andrew Garfield has received several award nominations and has won prestigious honors for his work in film and theater. Here's a brief overview of some of the awards won by Andrew Garfield:

  1. **Tony Award (2018):**
     - Garfield won the Tony Award for Best Actor in a Play for his performance as Prior Walter in the National Theatre's production of "Angels in America."
  
  2. **Academy Award Nomination (2017):**
     - Garfield received an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor for his role as Desmond Doss in "Hacksaw Ridge" (2016).
  
  3. **BAFTA Award Nomination (2017):**
     - He was nominated for the BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role for "Hacksaw Ridge."
